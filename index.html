While we can successfully restore most vinyl records, some damage may be beyond repair. Factors like the severity of scratches, degree of warping, and overall condition affect restoration potential. We offer free assessments and will always provide honest feedback about what results you can expect before any work begins.

Standard restoration projects typically take 7-10 business days to complete. More complex projects or rare/valuable records that require special handling may take 2-3 weeks. Rush services are available for an additional fee, with 48-72 hour turnaround possible for most standard projects.

Yes, we purchase quality used vinyl records both individually and as collections. Our buying process involves a thorough assessment of condition, rarity, and market demand. We pride ourselves on offering fair, competitive prices and can provide either cash or store credit at a higher rate.

We follow the standard Goldmine grading system (Mint, Near Mint, Very Good Plus, Very Good, Good, Fair, Poor) with meticulous attention to detail. Each record is visually inspected, play-tested, and honestly described. We err on the side of caution in our grading to ensure customer satisfaction.
